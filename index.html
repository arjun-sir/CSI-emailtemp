DISCOVER.JS

A series of technical workshops

The Computer Society of India brings to you Discover.js, a series of technical workshops where you get to make an actual take home project for your portfolio!

Discover.js consists of three workshops spanning over a course of 3 days covering the basics of javascript, next.js and its implementations.

Join us on the 8th, 11th and 15th of February, 2023 for exciting workshops.

On day 1, we'll introduce you to the world of js and dive deeper into next.js during the next 2 days. Finally, build your very own blog website!
